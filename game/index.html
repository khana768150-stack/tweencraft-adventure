How to Play
👈👉

Move Your Character

Use the Left / Right arrow buttons to walk. On desktop, use keyboard arrow keys.

⬆️

Jump

Tap the purple JUMP button (or press Space / Up arrow) to leap over enemies and gaps.

🪙

Collect Coins

Grab all yellow coins to earn stars. More coins = more stars at end of level!

🚩

Reach the Flag

Touch the green flag at the end of each level to win. Avoid enemies and pits!

💜

Lives

You start with 3 hearts. Touching an enemy or falling costs one life. Lose all 3 and it's game over!
